Scamazing Ball Game

Developed by: Hesham Jabr (XMRGroup)

Game instructions:

You will hit the ball towards the door and it will show you information about internet fraud.

At the end of the game you will answer a question, if you answer correctly you will win the game.

Game controller:
Left arrow: Move the paddle to the left
Right arrow: Move the paddle to the right.
Space: To hide the window and continue the game.
